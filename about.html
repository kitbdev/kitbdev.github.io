Kit Bishop

My name is Kit Bishop (full: Ian Christopher Bishop).
I am an experienced game developer with a passion for developing interactive experiences.

Over the past few years, I have been working professionally as a Software Developer, and while I enjoy writing good code, I wanted to shift to a field that I could be more passionate about than Finance, like Game Development and Virtual Reality.
I want to get into the VR industry and be able to develop experiences for VR full-time.

I have been interested in creating games since I was in High School in 2013. I started by making windows console games in C#, including an impossible hangman game and a procedurally generated turn-based roguelike. I would work on these games while I was waiting for the rest of the programming class to catch up to me.
After that, I discovered Game Jams like Ludum Dare and started making games to put online.
More on Game Jams.

In December of 2017, I graduated from Florida Polytechnic University with a Bachelor in Computer Science. From there I became a Contractor with Revature which got me a position as a Software Developer Engineer in Test at Finra. In February 2021 I completed XRTerra's XR Developer Bootcamp to help get into the VR industry.

Others have described me as easygoing, creative, and motivated. I am usually a quiet person but I strive to be helpful, innovative, and efficient.

I am currently 24 years old.
I am nonbinary, and my pronouns are they/them or she/her. Previously I can be found as Ian Bishop, and I am still undergoing the legal process of updating things.
Hobbies I enjoy include reading, taking walks, and learning French. Some of my favorite games are The Outer Wilds, Half Life Alyx, Skyrim, and Star Wars: Battefront 2.

Currently, I am located in Orlando, Florida but I am open to moving pretty much anywhere.
I am seeking a VR developer position.

Feel free to contact me if you have questions, offers, or just want to chat!
